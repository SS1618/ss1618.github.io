Color Rendition of Prokudin-Gorskii Collection

Background

The Prokudin-Gorskii Collection consists of photographs taken by Russian chemist and photographer, Sergei Mikhailovich Prokudin-Gorskii. For each scene, he captured three exposures with red, green, and blue filters. These photos were purchased by the Library of Congress in 1948.

Goal

The goal of this project is to write a program that can ingest RGB glass plate images and produce a colorized rendition of the scene.

Registration

In order to construct a color version of the scenes, the three RGB glass plate images needed to be overlayed. Unfortuantely, they are not aligned with each other, so overlaying them would just create a strange image with discordant colors.
